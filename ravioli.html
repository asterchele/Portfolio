Fried Ravioli

Ingredients

  • Your choice of ravioli (I recommend cheese)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 cups Italian bread crumbs
  • 1/4 parmesean cheese
  • Fresh Basil

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350°F for 5 minutes
  2. Mix parmesean and Italian bread crumbs together in a bowl. Place flour and whisked eggs into their own bowls
  3. Dip ravioli into flour, then into eggs and lastly into the Italian bread crumbs mixture
  4. Set air fryer to 350°F and cook raviolis for 6 minutes
  5. Flip raviolis onto other side. Cook for another 5 minutes at 350°F
  6. Remove from air fryer, top with fresh basil, and serve with warm marinara sauce!
