Welcome to College Eatz

The place where we ball on a budget

Hi! I'm Alex Sterchele and welcome to College Eatz. As a college student cooking on my own, I wanted to create a platfrom to share my tips and tricks I've learned along the way. College Eatz is focused on providing cooking recipes with my nifty air fryer, reviews on products from my favorite grocery store, Trader Joes, and gallery of my finished creations.

College Eatz is a destination for students to reference when looking for cheap, easy recipes that fulfill their lunches, dinners, or late night bites.
