Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup Pesto
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/2 sliced large tomato
  • Fresh Basil
  • 1/4 cup Balsamic Vinegar

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350°F for 5 minutes
  2. Roll out pizza dough into circular shape to fit into air fryer basket
  3. Spread olive oil on side of dough that will touch the basket
  4. Set air fryer to 350°F and dough cook 4 minutes
  5. Spread olive oil on top of dough and flip, cooking for another 2 minutes
  6. First add a layer of pesto, then mozzarella cheese, tomatoes and basil. Cook for 6 minutes at 370°F
  7. Add balsamic vinegar to saucepan and cook on medium heat and bring to a boil. Once it boils reduce the heat to medium-low to let simmer, occasionally stirring. The reduction will be ready when it can coat the back of the spoon you're using to mix
  8. Remove pizza from air fryer and place onto a plate. Drizzle with the balsamic reduction to top it off
