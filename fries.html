Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients

  • 1 Sweet Potato
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400°F for 3 minutes
  2. Peel sweet potato and cut into 1/4 inch thick sticks the long way
  3. Move sweet potato sticks into a bowl adding olive oil, garlic powder, parsley, paparika, salt, and pepper mixing thoroughly
  4. Set air fryer to 400°F and spray basket with nonstick cooking spray
  5. Place sweet potatoes in the air fryer and cook for 8 minutes
  6. Remove basket and turn fries
  7. Cook another 6-7 minutes at 400°F
  8. Place sweet potato fries onto plate and enjoy!
