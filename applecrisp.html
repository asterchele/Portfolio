Apple Crisp

Ingredients

  • 1 Apple
  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup or honey
  • 1 cup vanilla ice cream
  • Fresh Basil

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350°F for 5 minutes
  2. Cut apple in half through the core. Spoon out the hard core of the apple
  3. Put butter in a bowl then into microwave and heat 10-15 seconds to melt
  4. Add oats, cinnamon, sugar, maple syrup or honey into bowl with melted butter, mixing thoroughly
  5. Put mixture into center of the apple of the spooned out core
  6. Spray air fryer basket with non-stick cooking spray and place apples halves in basket
  7. Set air fryer to 350°F and let apple cook for about 14 minutes
  8. Remove apples from basket into a bowl and top with vanilla ice cream. Enjoy!
