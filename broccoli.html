Roasted Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli
  • olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup parmesean cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350°F for 5 minutes
  2. Cut head of broccoli into bite sized pieces
  3. Move broccoli into bowl. Add in garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper, and parmesean cheese
  4. Set air fryer to 350°F and cook broccoli for 8 minutes
  5. Remove basket and flip broccoli. Cook for another 6 minutes at 350°F
  6. Remove broccoli from air fryer and place onto a plate. Enjoy!
