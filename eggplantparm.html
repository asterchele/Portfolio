Eggplant Parm

Ingredients

  • 1 Eggplant
  • 1 cup Flour
  • 2 cups of Italian Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Parmesean Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370°F for 5 minutes
  2. Cut eggplant into 1/2" circular slices
  3. Put flour, eggs, and Italian bread crumbs into their own individual bowls. Add parmesean cheese to Italian bread crumbs and mix eggs in their own bowl
  4. Place eggplant into flour, then dip in egg and lastly cover in Italian bread crumbs. Make sure eggplant is evenly covered for each time it is dipped
  5. Spray air fryer basket with non-stick cooking spray and place eggplant in basket. Do not stack the eggplant on top of each other as this will not allow them to cook as fast
  6. Set air fryer to 370°F and let eggplant cook for about 8 minutes until golden
  7. Flip eggplant and allow to cook on the other side for 4 minutes
  8. Spread about 1 tablespoon of marinara sauce on eggplant topped with mozzarella and fresh basil. Cook another 2 minutes at 370°F
  9. Place onto plate and enjoy with pasta or on its own!
