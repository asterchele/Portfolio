Crispy Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 1 package of chicken wings
  • 3 tablespoons baking powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Buffalo Sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400°F for 5 minutes
  2. Remove chicken wings from package. Wash them with water and dry with paper towels
  3. Move chicken wings to bowl adding in salt, pepper, garlic powder and baking powder. Mix evenly
  4. Place wings in air fryer, cooking at 400°F for 10 minutes
  5. Remove basket and flip chicken wings. Bake at 400°F for another 8-10 minutes
  6. Put finished wings into bowl and add buffalo sauce. Toss until wings are evenly covered
  7. Place onto plate and enjoy!
